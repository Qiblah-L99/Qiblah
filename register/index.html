What We Offer

Everything the
community needs

From live prayer countdowns to community services — Qiblah connects Muslims in East London with their local mosques.

Live Prayer Times
Real-time Jamaah countdowns for Fajr, Zuhr, Asr, Maghrib and Isha. Always accurate, updated by the mosque directly.
Nearby Mosques
GPS-powered discovery. Find the nearest mosque to you instantly.
Community Services
Discover Quran classes, youth clubs, welfare services, and community programmes near you.
Online Courses
Access Islamic learning from SeekersGuidance, Yaqeen Institute, Al-Maghrib and more — all in one directory.
